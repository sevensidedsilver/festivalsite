Play With Fire

Rhapsody loves fire.
From fire spinning, to pyrotechnics, to building massive burnable structures -
We can't seem to get enough.
This love doesn't come without it's heartaches.
Please read though this page to ensure we have the safest
Experience possible with our fire artists.
If you plan on spinning fire yourself:
You must attend a safety workshop and have a wristband
Before lighting up.
All pyrotechnics must be registered and approved at the gate.

Fire Safety Rules

For Everyone Attending Rhapsody

1. Do not enter fire performance perimeters.

2. No fire outside of fire performance perimeters.

3. All equipment must be inspected and approved before performing.

4. Absolutely no open fuel dumps. Sealed ammo cans only!

5. At least one trained safety must be present for each performance.

6. Gasoline must be kept in red gas cans.

7. Absolutely no gasoline on any kind of fire. It's for generators only!

8. No ground fires. Fire pits must be approved and have 10 ft of clearance.
